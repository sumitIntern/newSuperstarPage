Account Deletion Guide
Thank you for being a part of our community. If you wish to delete your account, please follow these simple steps :
    1. Open the App :
    Launch the Superstarr on your mobile device.
    2. Go to Account Settings :
    Navigate to the 'Account' or 'Profile' section within the app.
    3. Locate Account Deletion Option :
    Look for the 'Delete Account' or 'Close Account' option. It might be under 'Settings' or 'Privacy.'
    4. Follow On-Screen Instructions :
    Once you've found the account deletion option, follow the on-screen instructions. You may need to provide additional information for verification purposes.
    5. Confirm Deletion :
    Confirm that you indeed want to delete your account. Be aware that this action is irreversible, and all data associated with your account will be permanently removed.
    6. Check for Confirmation :
    After completing the deletion process, check your email for a confirmation message. This ensures that the account deletion was successful.
    Note : Deleting your account will result in the loss of all data, including purchases, preferences, and other account-related information.
